General comments about Homework 17th October

Feedback on HW1

  • Generally good. Average was 34/40.
  • There are always multiple ways to solve something: they should all give the same answer! There were some really good ways of solving things that were quicker and easier than the way I would have done it.
  • Try different ways if you have time

Suggestions

  • Try to avoid putting in the 'numbers' into your equations until the end. It's easier to check limiting cases and you'll make less calculator errors. Also if the answer is wrong and you put in numbers at the beginning, it's extremely hard to grade and the grader may not give you as many points as you deserve.
  • Whenever you get to an answer, make sure it makes physical sense. Check units. Check limiting cases (i.e. what do you expect to happen when something becomes big or small, does this match with your answer?).
  • Don't be afraid to use more paper! It's easier to grade if you lay things out logically and in-order and write big/neat (and less likely for the grader to miss your working). Try not to skip steps to save space, as these could cost you in points.
